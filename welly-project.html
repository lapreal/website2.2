welly.

“1 in 4 people will be affected by mental health issues at some point in their life.”
- world health organization

Our prompt was to design a digital home assistant. After much research and ideation, I decided to design digital assistant that makes common mental wellness practices easily accessible on a daily basis. Why? Because increased daily screen time, working longer hours, fear of the future, uncertainty of self, relationships, and financial stresses are creating a mental pandemic of debilitating anxiety and depression. While our project was mentored by designers at google, we conducted brand studys of these three tech companies: Facebook, Amazon, and Google.

Through this process I knew Google’s brand promise best matched my design problem and potential solution. Sketching out the details of my final design concept helped me understand the details and features that would come to shape my final CAD and renders. Researching the postitive effects light and scent have on mental wellness influenced the features of my final concept.
