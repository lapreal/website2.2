Tala.

As part of an interdisciplinary innovation fellowship, I worked with a group of 6 other students from fields including mechanical engineering, computer science, marketing, and entreprenuership. We came together to create, design, and develop an IoT device and develop a business plan that would allow us to bring the product to market. Our product will decrease the anxiety that can come to climbers while dealing with the lack of quality communication with their belayers. We believe doing so will help to maximize the joy and decrease the frustration of climbing.

Our product is a bluetooth radio designed for rock climbers. The prototype will be built with Solidworks, 3D printing, Kicad PCB software, hand soldering, sewing, and silicone molds. We have several outdoor retailers and rock climbing gyms lined up ready to have us come talk to and test our prototype with their customers. We will collect this data using online surveys and advertising as well as in person interviews. My role in this project was industrial design, ux/ui design, and branding.
