trio.

I set out to design a toothbrush holder that is easy to clean in a pinch. After conducting user research on toothbrushes, I discovered one serious problem in holders: They are really gross because users hate cleaning them. Users primarily brush their teeth at times when they are likely in a rush to get somewhere (to work or to sleep).

After asking users for feedback on these designs, using milled polystyrene models, I found the majority of users liked the size of the space saver concept (the one in the middle in the image below). I also found they did not like the idea of their toothbrush head laying down on any kind of surface. They did like the way the family caddy concept (the one on the left in the image below) held the brushes and the aesthics of the concept. I listened and combined the best of both worlds.
